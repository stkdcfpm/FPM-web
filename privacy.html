3. How the AI chat works

The chat assistant on this site is powered by Anthropic's Claude API. When you send a message, your conversation history is transmitted to Anthropic's servers to generate a response. Once you confirm your enquiry, the full transcript and your contact details are delivered to FPM via Web3Forms (our email delivery provider).

Your conversation data is not stored in your browser (no cookies, no localStorage). It exists only in memory for the duration of your session and is cleared when you close or refresh the page.

6. How long we keep your data

  • In the AI chat: session memory only — cleared when you leave the page. No data is written to your device.
  • At Anthropic: up to 30 days from the time of your conversation, then automatically deleted.
  • At FPM (email inbox): enquiry emails are retained for as long as necessary to manage your enquiry and any resulting business relationship, typically up to 6 years in line with standard business record-keeping practice.
